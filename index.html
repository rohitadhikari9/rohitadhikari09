05 · Projects

Projects

🌍
Landslide Susceptibility Mapping of Sindhupalchok using GIS-based AHP
Developed a GIS-based landslide susceptibility model for Sindhupalchok District using the Analytical Hierarchy Process (AHP). Integrated spatial factors including slope, rainfall, geology, drainage density, TWI, land use/land cover, and proximity analysis using ArcGIS. Validated results using ROC-AUC analysis with historical landslide inventory data.
Tools: ArcGIS, GIS Analysis, AHP
Role: GIS Analysis, Mapping, Data Processing & Report Writing
AHPArcGISHazard MappingROC-AUCSindhupalchok

Conducted a research-based study on Artificial Intelligence and Automated Valuation Models (AVMs) in property valuation systems. Explored AI applications in real estate appraisal, valuation consistency, efficiency, and transparency with focus on Nepalese perspectives.
