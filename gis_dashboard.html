Key Insights
🏔 Himalaya Dominance
Solukhumbu & Kaski account for 63% of adventure tourism. EBC trek drives ~380K visitors annually — NPR 28B in economic activity.
SolukhumbuKaskiEBC
🛕 Heritage Concentration
Bagmati Province holds 18 of Nepal's 29 UNESCO sites. Kathmandu Valley receives 520K visitors — 47% of national total — in ~2% of land area.
UNESCOBagmatiCultural
📈 Untapped Growth
Karnali & Sudurpashchim show lowest density — significant ecotourism potential. Rara Lake & Api Nampa corridors remain largely undeveloped.
EcotourismKarnali
📅 Seasonality Pattern
Peak: Oct–Nov & Mar–Apr. Monsoon Jul–Aug sees 68% drop. Shoulder season promotion could yield 200K+ additional annual visitors.
🗺 GeoJSON Pre-Processing
Douglas-Peucker simplification (tolerance 0.005°) applied in QGIS. Topology validated. File size reduced ~68% for web performance.
